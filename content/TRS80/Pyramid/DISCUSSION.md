# Brief history (from the wikipedia)
  - 1976 Will Crowther PDP-10 mainframe about 700 lines of fortran and 700 lines of data. link mamoth cave
  - 1977 Don Woods extended the original source to 3000 lines of fortran and 1800 lines of data. link
    - Pirate (mummy) to put treasures in the maze
    - objects within objects (bird in the box)
    - scoring
  - 1977 James Gillogly ported the original to BSD. Find in linux as part of "bsdgames"
  - Several versions for different platforms. Robert Arnstein transposed it to Pyramid 2000
    for the TRS80 in 1979. Two versions: level 1 and level 2
  - Translated Pyramid 2000 to TRS80 CoCo in 1982 -- where I first played
